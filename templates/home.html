QUBIP MPU chain

The QUBIP MPU chain is used to sign end-entity certificates for the MPU device, using MLDSA65/ED25519 composite algorithm. It is used to secure the communication between the MPU and the MCU.

Root CA

The Root CA is the root of trust for PKI. It is used to sign the intermediate CAs and is not intended to be used for end-entity certificates.
